Cycle Mode

 

The first thing to do when you open MultiClip is to decide whether or not to enable the “Cycle Mode”.

 

When Cycle Mode is ON:

- The program will focus onto the next active clip every time a copy or paste is successfully executed.

- You can focus onto any active clip by pressing the hotkey CTRL + NUMBER (where NUMBER can be any key from 1 to 5). Press INSERT to copy or paste in COPY or PASTE mode, respectively. Of course, make use of the graphical interface to manage your clipboard at your wish.

- Always be aware of whether you are in COPY or PASTE mode, especially when Switch Mode is on.

 

When Cycle Mode is OFF:

- All of the clips will generally be out of focus. INSERT key is disabled. When CTRL + NUMBER is pressed, a copy or paste, depending on the active mode, will be executed using the clip specified by NUMBER.

 

When the Cycle Mode is either ON or OFF:

Press CTRL + INSERT, or use the graphical interface to toggle between COPY and PASTE modes.
